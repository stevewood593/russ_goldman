Russ Goldman

Jazz Saxophonist, Composer

Accomplished professional musician with 10 years of experience on the New York jazz scene. Leader of the Russ Goldman Quintet, and a member of the Wayne Krantz Octet. Assistant Professor of Music at the Manhattan School of Music.
