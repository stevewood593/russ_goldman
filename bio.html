Biography

A veteran of the New York jazz scene, Russ Goldman has been performing, teaching, and recording for the past 14 years. He has a B.A. in music from the University of Michigan and an M.S. in Music Composition from the Manhattan School of Music. Much in demand as a sideman and solo artist, Russ has also been written up in The New York Times, The Washington Post, and other publications. He teaches regularly at Rutgers University and gigs frequently throughout NYC and beyond.
